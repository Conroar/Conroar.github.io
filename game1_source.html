Captain's Handbook

MEAN (The Average)

Add all numbers, then divide by the count.

Ex: 2, 3, 4 -> 2+3+4 = 9. 9 ÷ 3 = 3.

MEDIAN (The Middle)

Sort numbers smallest to largest. The middle one is the median.

Ex: 2, 5, 1 -> 1, 2, 5 -> Middle is 2.

MODE (The Most)

The number that appears most often.

Ex: 3, 5, 3, 9 -> 3 is the Mode.

RANGE (The Distance)

Subtract the smallest number from the largest number.

Ex: 2, 5, 9 -> 9 - 2 = 7.
