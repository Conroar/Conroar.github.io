About Me

Hello, and welcome to my corner of the internet! My name is Connor Boyer, but you might know me better as Conroar. I recently graduated from the College of Saint Benedict’s and Saint John’s University with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science, complemented by a minor in Art. My journey in the digital world has led me to self-teach game design using C++ and GML, while also diving into the realms of art and animation with tools like Clip Studio Paint EX and Krita Studio.


For my senior capstone project, I explored the intriguing intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Augmented Reality, delving into how these technologies will reshape societal interactions. My coding adventures primarily involve GML, C++, Java, and Python, but I also have a foundational understanding of Tensorflow, Ruby, C#, and HTML.


In addition to my tech pursuits, I create content across YouTube, Twitch, and social media — covering gaming, creative projects, and the occasional vlogs. My passion for gaming is deep-rooted; I coached the Rainbow Six Siege team to two National Championships and led my team in Super Smash Bros Ultimate. My gaming interests are vast, spanning MMORPGs and Simulation games, with favorites like Animal Crossing New Horizons, Genshin Impact, and FFXIV.


When I’m not engrossed in the digital world, you’ll find me drawing, arranging music, or soaking in new tunes and skills. Whether it’s through games or art, I’m always on the lookout for new adventures and experiences. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope to share more of my journey with you soon!
